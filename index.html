History

Located in the heart of Orlando, Florida, Panino Perfetto is a culinary haven with origins dating back to the mesmerizing thoroughfares of Sicily, Italy, where it was conceived in the early 1960s. The narrative surrounding Panino Perfetto serves as evidence of an enduring passion for genuine Italian gastronomy.

Giovanni Russo, our founder, instigated a culinary revolution in the picturesque village of Sicilia where the company was established through the creation of exquisite paninis and pasta dishes. Demonstrating an unwavering dedication to traditional recipes that had been passed down through the generations, Giovanni crafted a menu that evoked the essence of Sicily.

In the present day, Panino Perfetto maintains its illustrious legacy from its inception. We take great pride in providing an experience reminiscent of Sicily, offering a diverse selection of delectable antipasti, paninis, and pasta delicacies that have been painstakingly crafted to evoke a sense of longing for the sunny shores of Sicily. Embark with us on a gustatory expedition that reveres our enduring heritage while savoring the delights of Italy.
